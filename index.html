librokwago

[noun] /'li.bro ku'ago/

In Filipino, "libro" means book, while "kwago" means an owl. That is to say, LIBRO KWAGO is the book of an owl. Alternatively, the name of Usha Cobrado's writing blog and personal website.

Mabuhay, mga manunulat.
Welcome to Libro Kwago, a personal writing blog of mine containting all my written works, such as poems, stories, one-shots, and etc. Let's read and write till the owls tell our tales!
